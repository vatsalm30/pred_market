# OmniPred — Product Context

## Users
Prediction market enthusiasts, casual traders, quant researchers. People who already have accounts on Polymarket and Kalshi and want to compare odds between them without manually switching tabs. Weekend warriors checking election and sports markets over coffee.

## Product Purpose
A free-to-use cross-platform comparison tool. Shows matched markets side-by-side, detects arbitrage opportunities, and surfaces pricing differences. Feels like it was built by someone who uses prediction markets themselves — not a corporate data product.

## Brand Voice
Sharp, curious, honest. No jargon. Not selling anything right now. Built to be useful first.

## Register
brand (landing page) + product (comparison tools)

## Anti-references
- SaaS pitch decks with pricing tables and "Get API Access" CTAs
- Bloomberg terminal aesthetics (too cold)
- Crypto-neon maximalism
- Generic fintech navy/gold

## Tone
Enthusiast-built. Like a well-crafted open-source tool that happens to look great.
